Follow this step to run the Unit Test:
1. Make sure you've installed XCode on your macbook
2. Extract Project, and then double click on 'MusicPlaylist.xcodeproj'
3. Input your signing & capabilities if needed
4. After the project opened on your xcode, Choose Scheme 'MusicPlaylist'
5. On Navbar Menu choose Product > Text or Command + U
6. The unit test will run automatically and wait until it done.
7. After it done, go to your side nav, and then choose 'Report Navigator' and click on 'Coverage' that showing the code coverage percentage, 
    it will show the detail code coverage result
8. Done, Let me know if you facing any problem, Thanks!
